<objective>
Goal: Extend PATS-Copy with tiered multi-trader monitoring — track the top 5 leaderboard traders simultaneously. Rank 2-5 trades are eligible for copying only when corroborated by at least 2 of 3 signals: Glint match, AI confidence ≥0.75, and orderbook buy pressure. Rank 1 retains unconditional copy priority and always supersedes watcher positions.

Purpose: Capture profitable opportunities even when the rank 1 leader is inactive, while maintaining the high-quality bar required for consistent wins. Rank-scaled sizing (rank 2=60%, rank 3=50%, rank 4=40%, rank 5=30%) limits exposure on lower-conviction signals.

Output: Bot monitors 5 wallets in parallel, copies rank 2-5 trades with corroboration gate, automatically defers to rank 1 on conflict.
</objective>

  When ConfirmationLayer evaluates it

  And the final size = base_allocation × rank_multiplier
  And log shows "Rank-scaled: rank=3 multiplier=0.50 size=$X"

AC-5: Rank 1 collision closes watcher positions
  Given an open watcher position exists for market M (from rank 2-5 trade)
  When rank 1 leader trades market M
  Then CopyExecutor first closes the watcher position
  Then opens a new full-size rank 1 position in the same market
  And log shows "Rank-1 collision: closing watcher position for [marketId], re-entering at rank-1 size"

AC-6: OrderbookChecker queries CLOB API correctly

  When OrderbookChecker.getBidPressure(tokenId) is called

  And returns bid/(bid+ask) ratio as a number 0-1
  And handles 404/timeout gracefully (returns null, treated as corroboration=false)
